This is where we'll share what's new in **StudentFlow** — feature releases, behind-the-scenes design notes, and the occasional study tip. No noise, just the good stuff.

## What is StudentFlow?

StudentFlow is an iOS app that brings your courses, assignments, and GPA into one calm, beautiful place. It's built around a simple idea: the tools you use to manage school should feel as good as they are useful — so you can spend less time wrangling tabs and more time actually studying.

## What we're working on

A few things on the horizon:

- **GPA calculator & degree progress** — plan the grades you need and watch your degree fill in.
- **Lock Screen Live Activities** — your next deadline, glanceable without unlocking.
- **Canvas sync** — bring your courses and assignments in automatically.
